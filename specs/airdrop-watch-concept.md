# Airdrop Watch Concept

## Purpose
Explore a new discovery surface inside LamboMoon focused on early tokenless projects, probable airdrops, and potential future drops.

This should be treated as a product concept first, not an immediate commitment to a full data product.

---

## Working Name
**Airdrop Watch**

Alternative labels:
- Potential Drops
- Tokenless Projects to Watch
- Drop Radar

"Airdrop Watch" is the clearest and most legible for users.

---

## Why This Fits LamboMoon
LamboMoon is about:
- early discovery
- community conviction
- finding things before they are obvious

Airdrops naturally fit because they are:
- early
- attention-driven
- research-heavy
- very shareable
- aligned with crypto discovery culture

This makes Airdrop Watch more on-brand than a generic paid listing system.

---

## Product Positioning
Airdrop Watch should not claim certainty.
It should help users track:
- tokenless projects
- likely airdrop candidates
- new ecosystem incentives
- projects worth monitoring early

That means the section is best framed as:
- **watchlist / radar / discovery**
not
- guaranteed airdrop claims

---

## Core Product Principle
Use:
- source transparency
- confidence labels
- editorial framing
- community boosting

Avoid:
- pretending every project will definitely airdrop
- low-trust speculation without labeling
- paid placement disguised as organic inclusion

---

## Best MVP Version
An Airdrop Watch item should have:
- project name
- ecosystem
- source
- confidence score
- status
- short "why it matters" note
- relevant links
- optional timing information if known

### Example fields
- `name`
- `ecosystem`
- `status` (`watching`, `testnet`, `campaign live`, `snapshot rumor`, etc.)
- `source`
- `confidence_score`
- `why_watch`
- `website_url`
- `x_url`
- `docs_url`
- `tasks_summary` (later)
- `airdrop_likelihood` (later, carefully)

---

## Section Ideas

### On homepage
- **Airdrop Watch** section with 3–6 cards
- editorial / radar style

### Dedicated page later
- `/airdrops`
- filter by ecosystem
- sort by confidence / freshness / boost count

### Project detail integration
If a project is on Airdrop Watch, show a label like:
- `Airdrop Watch`
- `Tokenless`
- `Potential Drop`

---

## Confidence Framework
Confidence should be explicit.

Suggested labels:
- **High** → strong evidence / campaign / credible signals
- **Medium** → likely but not confirmed
- **Low** → interesting rumor / early candidate / worth watching

This is important for trust.

---

## Source Framework
Airdrop Watch entries should always show source.

Examples:
- manual curation
- CryptoRank
- ecosystem campaign page
- docs / official announcements
- community monitoring

This helps LamboMoon feel credible instead of speculative.

---

## Monetization View
At least initially, this should be **organic and not paid**.

Why:
- trust matters a lot
- airdrop hunters are skeptical
- hidden paid inclusion would damage credibility fast

If monetization happens later, it should be:
- separate
- clearly labeled
- not mixed into organic watch picks

---

## Risks

### 1. False certainty
Users may assume every entry will result in a drop.

Mitigation:
- confidence labels
- explicit wording
- source transparency

### 2. Low-quality speculation
If the bar is too low, the section becomes noisy.

Mitigation:
- curator review
- confidence scoring
- clear status labels

### 3. Maintenance burden
Airdrop opportunities change quickly.

Mitigation:
- keep the MVP lightweight
- focus on watch-worthy projects, not exhaustive task tracking yet

---

## Recommended MVP Scope
Do first:
- Airdrop Watch concept section
- small set of curated entries
- source + confidence labels
- strong editorial framing

Do later:
- task lists
- eligibility hints
- reminders
- campaign tracking
- dedicated airdrop workflow

---

## Recommendation
This is a strong future feature and a better immediate fit than sponsored placements.

Why:
- more useful
- more aligned with LamboMoon’s identity
- discovery-first
- trust-preserving if labeled correctly

**Recommendation:** pursue as an organic product feature once the broader data ingestion and curation layers are a bit more mature.
