# Homepage Wording Recommendations

## Current Problem
We should avoid claims that imply the whole dataset is manually curated if it is not.

LamboMoon should sound:
- ambitious
- discovery-focused
- credible
- community-driven

---

## Recommended Positioning

### Core line
**Index broadly. Curate selectively. Boost the best.**

Alternative:
**Community-curated crypto discovery for the next wave of tokens.**

---

## Safer Stat Alternatives

### Instead of
- `2,500+ Projects Tracked`

### Use one of
- `Thousands of cryptos indexed`
- `New and upcoming cryptos tracked`
- `Community-curated crypto discovery`
- `Early signals across the crypto universe`

---

## Recommended Homepage Stat Row

### Option A — safest now
- **Thousands** → cryptos indexed
- **Community** → boosts and signals
- **Fresh** → new + upcoming tokens

### Option B — more product-forward
- **Thousands indexed**
- **Boost-driven ranking**
- **New + upcoming tokens**

### Option C — if we want less numeric bravado
- **Broad coverage**
- **Community curation**
- **Fresh discovery**

---

## Recommended Hero Direction

### Existing theme
The current product vibe is fun / moonshot / discovery.
That should stay.

### Recommended hero support copy
- "Discover the next crypto gems before they take off"
- "Community-boosted crypto discovery"
- "Find new and upcoming tokens before they hit the mainstream"

---

## Recommended Trust Framing

LamboMoon should feel like:
- Product Hunt for crypto
- plus market context
- plus community conviction

Good lines:
- "Boost projects you believe in"
- "Track momentum, not just market cap"
- "Spot early signals before the crowd"

---

## Suggested Immediate Copy Changes

### Stat block
Replace hard numeric marketing with:
- `Thousands indexed`
- `Boost-driven discovery`
- `New + upcoming tokens`

### Section ordering
Put community-forward sections higher:
1. hero
2. trending alpha
3. boosted / curated discovery
4. biggest movers

This reinforces that LamboMoon is about *signal*, not just price movement.
